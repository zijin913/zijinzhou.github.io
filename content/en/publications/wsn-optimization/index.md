abstract: "This paper presents a comparative study of nature-inspired metaheuristics for optimizing coverage in Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN). We evaluate Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO), Grey Wolf Optimizer (GWO), and Genetic Algorithms (GA) under binary sensing models with obstacle constraints. Our results demonstrate that the proposed hybrid approach achieves up to 86% coverage efficiency, outperforming standard baselines in complex environments."
